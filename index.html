Pedro Núñez

I am a postdoc working in algebraic geometry in the group of Jungkai Alfred Chen at NTU in Taipei, Taiwan. Before I was a PhD student of Stefan Kebekus at the University of Freiburg.

Here is a picture of myself (courtesy of MFO) and here is my CV.

Research interests

Birational classification of algebraic varieties and derived categories of algebraic varieties. More specifically:

  • Campana's birational classification program and differential forms therein.
  • Semiorthogonal decomopositions and their relation to the minimal model program.
